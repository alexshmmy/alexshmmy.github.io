Short bio

Alexandros Nikou received the MEng degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering and the MSc degree in Automatic Control Systems and Robotics both from National Technical University of Athens (NTUA) in 2012 and 2014 respectively. In 2019, he received the PhD in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from KTH Royal Institute of Technology. He is currently researcher in Ericsson HQ in Stockholm, Sweden. His research interests include AI, robotics and automation.
